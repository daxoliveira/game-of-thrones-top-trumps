Top Trumps

Welcome to Game of Thrones Top Trumps

Follow these slides for a quick guide on how to play this game!

Step 1

The first version of the game includes a deck of 10 cards. once you click the "Game Start" button the deck will be evenly distributed between two adversaries, the computer and you, the player.

Step 2

The game will be ready to start when one card facing up (player) against another one facing down (the computer).

Step 3

Each card will have three properties with numerical values. The player will choose a property from his/her card (by clicking on it) to be compared against the facedown card's same property. The player wins the round if he/she has the highest property value.

Step 4

Once one of the adversaries has won all the cards, he or she will be the game winner. Now, click on the "Game" tab and let's play!!!
